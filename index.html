Metaphysics of Time
The metaphysics of time delves into the philosophical questions surrounding the nature of time itself. Central to this exploration is the debate between the theories of "eternalism" and "presentism." Eternalists argue that past, present, and future events all exist simultaneously, while presentists assert that only the present moment is real. This debate not only challenges our fundamental understanding of time but also forces us to reconsider our perceptions of reality and existence.
