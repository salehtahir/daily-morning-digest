💡 Did You Know?

Did you know that the iconic tune "Happy Birthday to You" has a fascinating historical origin? In 1893, Patty Hill, a kindergarten teacher, co-wrote the melody with her sister Mildred for a tune called "Good Morning to All." The song's transformation into the birthday anthem we know today showcases how everyday moments of creativity can resonate through generations, adding layers of joy and connection to our lives. It's incredible to think that a simple classroom melody has become a universal symbol of celebration and togetherness, proving that sometimes the most enduring legacies are born from the simplest beginnings.
