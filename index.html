💡 Did You Know?

In the early 1900s, renowned artist Frida Kahlo faced a significant turning point in her life after a devastating bus accident left her with severe injuries. During her recovery, confined to a body cast, Kahlo discovered her passion for painting and began creating the iconic self-portraits that would define her artistic legacy. This unexpected twist of fate not only propelled Kahlo to international acclaim but also revolutionized the art world by showcasing the raw emotions and personal struggles of a remarkable woman artist. Who knew that such a tragic event could give birth to one of the most influential artists of the 20th century?
