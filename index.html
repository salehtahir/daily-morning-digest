🧠 Philosophical Insight

Epistemology: The Nature of Knowledge
Epistemology deals with the nature of knowledge itself - what it is, how it is acquired, and how it relates to truth. This branch of philosophy delves into questions about the boundaries of knowledge, the sources of knowledge, and the criteria for justifying beliefs. Epistemologists explore concepts like perception, reason, memory, and testimony to understand how we come to know what we claim to know.
