Ethical Dilemmas
** The Trolley Problem The Trolley Problem is a philosophical thought experiment that presents a moral dilemma. It asks whether it is more ethical to actively cause harm to one person to save many others, or to abstain from action, allowing harm to come to more individuals. This dilemma delves into the complexities of moral decision-making, the value of individual lives versus the greater good, and the implications of our choices on a deeper level of ethics.
Are there universal moral principles that can guide us in making difficult decisions, or are ethics ultimately subjective and context-dependent?
